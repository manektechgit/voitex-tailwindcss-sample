IVR Branch

Automatically and intelligently guide each caller to the right place

Turn routine customer inquiries into deeper service experiences that boost satisfaction. All while reducing operating overhead.

Rolodex & Routing

Rolodex & Routing

  • Save customer names, numbers, passwords and more in our cloud contact center

  • Use our prebuilt branch system, or customize yours for call routing—no code required

  • Automatically pull up a customer’s complete profile based on their caller ID number

  • Assign and track per-caller metrics including points, labels, status, surveys, and more

Prompts & Incentives

Prompts & Incentives

  • Systematically request caller information, and record responses, using Speech to Text

  • Automatically merge newly collected caller data with their existing customer profile

  • Create custom incentives for callers based on their call volume and/or call duration

  • Set unlimited caller login passcodes and grant access permissions on a granular level

Branching & Transfers

  • Customize your extension/ branch lists based on your team structure and/or services

  • Intelligently route individual callers through the correct flow of branches/agents

  • Transfer live calls to any extension for seamless handoff between system and agents

  • Call out from any phone line and your individual extension will appear on caller ID

Monitoring & Logging

Pre-record unlimited audio messages to play when callers reach any specific branch

Listen in to any calls, callers, and/or branches without alerting any of the call’s parties

Log customers’ caller ID and call duration, and save detailed logs for calls and routing

Track which branches each customer ‘visited’ and to which recordings they listened

Destinations & Keywords

Destinations & Keywords

  • Categorize your agents based on their focus, and route calls that require those skills

  • Assign inputs, functions, and keywords per branch/agent to auto-route the right calls

  • Create any connected matrix of branches to create your custom unified IVR system

  • Templatize call flows to enable instant, accurate routing of common calls in the future

Walloffs & Registrations

Walloffs & Registrations

  • Set passwords to protect your full or partial IVR system against unauthorized access

  • Restrict specific branches by time or caller, and allow or disallow password override

  • Create branches or events accessible exclusively by pre-registered guests/callers

  • Pre-register guests for branches/events using the Voitex portal or over the phone
